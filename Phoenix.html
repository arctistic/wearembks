Phoenix

Under this project we are involved in the sanitation and cleanliness sector in the Hills. We organize cleanliness campaigns and awareness camps to make people more responsible towards their civic duties of keeping their place clean. We have launched a pilot project with Darjeeling Municipality to curb the menace of trash and garbage in the town.
