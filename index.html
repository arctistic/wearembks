Welcome to Mukh Bandh Kaam Suru

Mukh Bandh Kaam Suru is a Public Charitable trust registered under the Indian Trust Act 1960 with the registration number IV-0401-00024/2017, in Darjeeling, West Bengal, India. MBKS is a Public Charitable Trust dedicated towards bringing back the lost essence of Darjeeling. We are involved in Education, Children Healthcare, Youth Empowerment, Poverty Alleviation, and Sanitation and Cleanliness sector.
