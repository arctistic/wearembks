Yuwa Uday

Gorkha Youth Summit

MBKS Trust, which is committed to bringing back the lost essence of our Hills and devoted to the furtherance of social, philosophical and intellectual inclination among the youths of the Hills, is hosted a youth based intellectual symposium titled the “Gorkha Youth Summit 2019 (GYS)”. It was programme built on the foundation of the likes of the Literary Fests, Ted Talks, World Economic Forum, Oxford Union Debates & numerous youth festivals uniquely organized for the social union and celebration of ideas, that these Hills have always been bereft of.

The GYS has the potential to become an annual event of great significance to make the youths a part of the intellectual discourse in this part of the country. We believe the exclusion, deprivation & regression our youths face or the chains of ignorance that have shackled them so far is a huge factor in itself for having a much needed advent of an intellectual reform to this place. We thus, due to utmost necessity for the all-round development of a young inquisitive mind, which deserves an equal opportunity to be at par with such other minds of the nation and that beyond our border, decided to host the first ever “Gorkha Youth Summit 2019” at the GORKHA DUKHA NIWARAK SAMMELAN (G.D.N.S) Auditorium, Darjeeling on 17th & 18th October, 2019. Various acclaimed youths and personalities of the Hills in the field of Education, Research, Environment, Art and Cinema, Music, Drama and Poetry, Literature, Sports, Politics, Journalism, Academics, Business and Social Field will grace the event and take part in the panel discussions and seminars over two days. Our only objective is to introduce such acclaimed achievers as true role models to our youths who do not have such people to idolize as we tend to be everything but a Gorkha! Such stories like theirs need to be very warmly celebrated by all.

We plan to make GYS an annual event of immense cultural, academic and intellectual significance for the Gorkha youths of not only our Hills but of the entire world over the years. We hope that you all will help support our noble cause.

Committe for Eradication of Drug Abuse and Rehabilitation (CEDAR)

We also have joined hands with the Darjeeling Police to form an empowered committee of different NGOs named Committee for Eradication of Drug Abuse and Rehabilitation (CEDAR) to combat the menace of drug abuse in the Hills and find curative measures for it.
