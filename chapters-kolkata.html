MBKS Chapters

Kolkata

Our Chapter in Kolkata was the first chapter that we opened outside Darjeeling. As our team has to visit the city on a regular basis for our different projects, we saw a need and opportunity to open a Chapter here.

Mr. Rounak Thapa is our Senior Representative in Kolkata. He is joined in the team by Mr. Sonam Thargay Dukpa, Mr. Sagrit Pradhan and Mr. Anurag Gurung. They are all students in various universities in Kolkata.

Our Kolkata Chapter is involved in various activities. It plays a major role in Project CHILD whereby the treatment of children under this project in Kolkata is the responsibility of our Chapter.

The Chapter is also the link between us and our partnership with Y-East, a social platform initiative of Techno India Group. We have opened the first chapter of Y-East outside Kolkata in Darjeeling and our Kolkata chapter plays a crucial role in maintaining this important relationship with them.

We were also felicitated by Kolkata Heroes Foundation with the 'We Are Heroes' Award in Kolkata for our work in various sectors. The Kolkata Chapter played a crucial role for this felicitation.
