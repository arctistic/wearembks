MBKS Chapters

Sikkim

Our Chapter in Sikkim is the first chapter that we opened outside West Bengal giving us a presence outside our state. This has given a boost to make MBKS Trust reach out nationally.

Mr. Sylvanas Sherpa is our Senior Representative in Sikkim. He is an Audit Officer in the Dprt. of Forest, Sikkim.

Our Sikkim Chapter is the bridge between us and Hazaar Sapana, an American NGO, run by the youngest daughter of Mrs. Dil Kumari Bhandari, the former parliamentarian from Sikkim. Hazaar Sapana is sponsoring the education of 12 children under Project MEFAI at Betten High School, Tukvar, Darjeeling and they also sponsored the building of a Library there.

Our Sikkim Chapter is flooding with ideas to spread the work and vision across the state. It helps us in raising awareness among the youths in the state about our various activities and is actively involved in other philanthropic activities.
