MBKS Education For All Initiative (MEFAI)

CHILD

Under this we bear the medical expenses of children belonging to financially week section of our society, suffering from chronic diseases.

We are currently looking after four children under it. Mr. Bhawishya Rai (19) of Dhooteriah TE who is suffering from double renal failure.

Master Ingso Subba (3) of Singtam TE who is suffering from a rare form of anaemia called Diamond-Blackfan syndrome, and which is the only recorded case of its kind in Darjeeling Hills.

Master Namsang Limbu (1) of Ropeway, Darjeeling, who suffers from Haemolytic Anaemia. All the cases are in their advanced stage of recovery/treatment due to our timely intervention.

We tasted our first success under Project CHILD last year when we successfully got Ms. Shriya Sarki (12) of Ropeway, Singamari, who had a brain tumour in her left cerebrum, operated upon at Bangalore and brought her back to Darjeeling in sound condition.
